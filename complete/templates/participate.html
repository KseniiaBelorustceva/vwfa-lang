Timing accuracy

This study involves timing, either for tachistoscopic display, for measurement of reaction time, or other functions. Timing accuracy can be degraded by other programs executing concurrently or by background network traffic to this machine (such as streaming real audio or real video). Please close all other programs that use an Internet or network connection such as mail clients and instant messaging applications.
